Congolese Traditional Wedding Pictures

Project details

  • Client: Eric & Irene Kabasele
  • Project date: 18 July, 2020

A small collection of photos taken for the client’s traditional wedding. The client expressed a need for the vibrance of colours in clothing, skin and a “memory-like” feel when editing the pictures. These photos were taken on the entry-level DSLR Canon EOS Rebel SL2 with an 18-55mm lens, then edited using Adobe Lightroom.
